Intro to iOS With Swift


Curious about getting started with iOS and Swift? Join us for a 2-hour demo where we will explore key iOS concepts and utilities like the camera, GPS system, and networking APIs. This is designed for beginners so if you do not have any programming experience, do not worry. If you want to follow along on your computer, make sure you bring a Mac with Xcode 7.3.
